ABOUT GAME

STAR CRUSADE:
FIGHT FOR THE
FUTURE.

Star Crusade: Fight For The Future is an
exciting space adventure that combines
elements of Location Based Game, P2E and
educational content. Walk the streets of
your city collecting resources, study
scientific phenomena or blockchain
innovations, fight real players with your
minion robots and get rewarded in the form
of $SCT token.

The first step towards the stars!
